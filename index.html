Welcome to MAINSOURCE METALFAB LLC!

MAINSOURCE METALFAB LLC is a full service steel contractor providing services in the New York/New Jersey area.

We specialize in fabrication of miscellaneous metal and structural steel for your most demanding construction needs.

Here at MAINSOURCE our company's goals are four-fold:

Provide quality work.
Strictly observe safety.
Meet or exceed schedules.
Keep prices competitive.

We strive to be pro-active in every stage of the job. Whether it's in submittals, in-house ACAD detailing, precise fabrication and on-time delivery. By controlling our downtime and proper coordination between fabrication and delivery, production loss will be dramatically cut.

Mainsource is MBE/DBE (Minority/Disadvantaged Business Enterprise) certified by different city and state agencies. We belong to the NJ Unified Certification Program. We are also a member of the AISC (American Institute of Steel Constructors), and our certification is in progress.
